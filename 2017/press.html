About the NASA International Space Apps Challenge

Since 2012, the NASA International Space Apps Challenge has been uniting people across the globe in collaborating to improve life on Earth and in space. Last year's event saw more than 15409 hackers participate from 161 cities in 61 countries, creating over 1300 projects!
Read the 2016 Mission Report
