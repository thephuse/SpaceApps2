About the NASA International Space Apps Challenge

Since 2012, the NASA International Space Apps Challenge has been uniting people across the globe in collaborating to improve life on Earth and in space. Last year's event saw 9047 participants in 83 cities create a total of 770 solutions. Read the 2013 Mission Report

In its first year running, the NASA International Space Apps Challenge Toronto had 110 participants in the adult event and 160 in the youth event. You can learn more about last year's here
