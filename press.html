An intense 48-hour weekend hackathon for developers, designers, engineers, entrepreneurs and enthusiasts. Teams will form and take on challenges developed by NASA that leverage the agency's extensive spacecraft, celestial and science data.

Competing and collaborating with 75 cities across the globe, we'll make our planet and universe accessible, visual, and fun to explore – and defend Toronto and Canada as leaders in innovation and science on the world stage.
