Space Apps - Beer & Post-Its Final Details!

One week to Beer and Post-Its!

It’s hard to believe that the main event is two weeks away, much less that we’ll be seeing your bright faces a week from today to discuss the challenges, start forming teams, and learning skills about things we’ll need to succeed in the weekend.

Where? When? What?

Where?
Hotel Ocho (map) - go to the top floor lobby, and you’ll see us there!
When?
6:30pm doors open, 7pm sharp start! We’ll wrap up by 8:30pm.
What do I need?
Bring your computer, anything you use to make notes, and your excitement. We’re going to have a great time, but to do that we require participation from everyone!

Thanks, JetCooper!

We’re very glad to have the JetCooper team facilitating this event and giving us a short talk on some ideas of things we should prepare for in the hackathon, and helping to facilitate discussions and the forming of teams throughout the evening!

Free After? Come to Yuri’s Night!

There aren’t many tickets left, so make sure if you’d like to come to Yuri’s Night that you buy a ticket for it. It’s going to be a great time with a space DJ, space speakers, a huge LEGO contest, and cosmo cocktails! We hope to have you all there!
