Where? When? What?

Where?
Hotel Ocho - go to the top floor lobby, and you’ll see us there!
When?
6:30pm doors open, 7pm sharp start! We’ll wrap up by 8:30pm.

You don't need your laptop. Bring anything you use to make notes, and your excitement. If you do bring a computer, make sure it's charged — We can't guarantee power outlets for everyone, or great WiFi for Beer & Post-Its.
