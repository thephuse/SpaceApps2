T-Minus 8 Days!

With just over a week to the event and our excitement building, we have a few updates prior to the event that we’d like to request your attention for:

This will allow us to invite people who are presently on the waiting list to come out to the event, and while we’d be sad you wouldn’t be able to make it, we know they’d appreciate it!

We still need your information!

If you haven’t already filled out the t-shirt request form we sent earlier, we’d request that you do so before midnight tonight for us to hold onto a shirt for you at the registration table. The form asks questions to help us out with determining skills for those attending, as well as questions from some of our sponsors and partners. Overall, we promise it won’t take more than 5 minutes of your time!
