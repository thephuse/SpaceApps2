Hang Tight!

Right now you’re on the waitlist for NASA’s Toronto Space Apps Challenge, which is happening this weekend from April 19-21. An event that was originally planned on having 50 people, we have 200 people confirmed and registered, and a waitlist of nearly 50 people. We’re trying to get as many people in as we can, but unfortunately there’s only so much we can do.

We want to make sure you hang tight while we do this and don’t fret. In the next couple days we’ll be emailing people who we can fit in one at a time. If we don’t get to you, there are two things to remember:

  • Please don’t show up on Friday. While we’d love to let everyone in, we can only allow people who have registered in time in on Friday. Everyone else will be sent home. Based on turnout on Friday we may email some people to come on Saturday morning, so we encourage you to keep your weekend free.
  • You can participate virtually! We know part of the experience is physically attending, but you can also participate from home virtually on any challenges. More information can be found here.

Please do let us know if you have any questions or anything else, and thank you for your patience!
