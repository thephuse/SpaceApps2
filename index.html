Space Apps Toronto 2013 Has Launched!

We’ve had an incredible weekend hacking alongside 9000 international participants on over 600 incredible projects, and on behalf of our local organizing team both locally and internationally, we’d like to thank everyone who came out to the event to work on our 26 amazing projects!

We feel honoured to have spent the weekend hacking with everyone, and are so proud of what everyone created together. You’ve all affected the way space data can be consumed and used in everyday life, made real research easier, and proved that Toronto is the place to be for innovation in technology and space.

If you weren’t with us we hope you’ll join us next year, but in the meantime we thought everyone should check out these incredible projects that were created over the weekend by our local Toronto team!
